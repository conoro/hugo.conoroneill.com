Old Chapel Drag Race - w00t !!

Posted by Conor O'Neill on Sunday, April 23, 2006

Bloody Brilliant, and we only watched the practice runs.

We positioned ourselves at the finish line on the advice of a steward (“they’ll reach max speed there”). It took a while to get going but the organisation of the event was really professional. Fionn (aged 9 months) screamed every time a bike also screamed across the line. Ois√≠n hid in a buggy, Shibs was unimpressed and Oscar went quiet.
